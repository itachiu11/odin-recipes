delcious tomato pie

Tomato pie is a savory, Southern summer staple featuring a flaky pastry crust filled with layers of sliced, fresh tomatoes, often with basil and green onion, and topped with a mixture of mayonnaise and shredded cheeses like mozzarella and cheddar. It is typically served warm or at room temperature, either as a light meal or a side dish, highlighting the sweet, juicy flavor of ripe, seasonal tomatoes.

    • Steps to make your tomato pie

    1. Add all the ingredients (besides the cheese and mayo) to the pie crust.
    2. Mix the cheese and mayo together and spread the mixture over the filling.
    3. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes, uncover, and bake for 30 more minutes.
